Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need to buy a home? +
Standard requirements include proof of identity, proof of income (last 3-6 months), credit report, and initial deposit evidence. Our agents provide a full checklist during your first consultation.
Are there hidden fees in the buying process? +
STACKLY prides itself on transparency. Beyond the property price, standard closing costs include title insurance, escrow fees, and inspections. We provide a full cost breakdown upfront.
How long does a typical closing take? +
Usually, the process takes 30 to 45 days. However, cash offers can sometimes close in as little as 10-14 days.
